## Getting Started

To connect an integration:

1. Navigate to your organization settings
2. Click on the **Integrations** tab
3. Find the integration you want to connect
4. Click **Connect** and follow the OAuth flow
5. Authorize the application to access your account

## Managing Integrations

Once connected, you can:

- **View connected integrations** - See all active integrations in your organization
- **Disconnect integrations** - Remove access by clicking **Disconnect**
- **Configure settings** - Adjust integration-specific settings as needed

## Security & Privacy

All integrations use OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication. Your credentials are encrypted and stored securely. We never store your passwords or access tokens in plain text.
