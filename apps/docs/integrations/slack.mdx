# Slack Integration

Connect your Epic Stack workspace to Slack to share notes and updates directly with your team channels.

## Overview

The Slack integration allows you to:

- Share notes to Slack channels
- Post updates and notifications to your team
- Collaborate seamlessly between Epic Stack and Slack
- Keep your team informed in real-time

## Setup Instructions

### 1. Create a Slack App

1. Go to [Slack API Dashboard](https://api.slack.com/apps)
2. Click **Create New App** and select **From scratch**
3. Name your app (e.g., "Epic Stack") and select your workspace
4. Go to **OAuth & Permissions** in the left sidebar
5. Under **Scopes**, add the following bot token scopes:
   - `channels:read` - Read channel information
   - `chat:write` - Post messages to channels
   - `channels:history` - Read channel history
   - `groups:read` - Read private channel information
6. Under **Redirect URLs**, add your callback URL: `https://your-domain.com/api/integrations/oauth/callback`
7. Copy your **Client ID** and **Client Secret** to your environment variables

### 2. Set Environment Variables

Add these to your `.env` file:

```bash
SLACK_CLIENT_ID=your_slack_client_id
SLACK_CLIENT_SECRET=your_slack_client_secret
INTEGRATION_ENCRYPTION_KEY=64_character_hex_string
```

### 3. Connect Your Slack Workspace

1. Go to your organization settings
2. Navigate to the **Integrations** tab
3. Find **Slack** and click **Connect**
4. You'll be redirected to Slack's authorization page
5. Select the workspace you want to connect
6. Click **Allow** to grant permissions

### 4. Select Channels

After connecting, you can select which channels to share notes to:

- Available channels will be displayed
- Select your preferred channels for note sharing
- Configure notification preferences

## Using the Integration

### Sharing Notes to Slack

1. Create or edit a note in Epic Stack
2. Look for the Slack sharing option
3. Select the channel(s) to share to
4. Click **Share to Slack**
5. The note will be posted as a formatted message

### Receiving Notifications

Configure which events trigger Slack notifications:

- New notes created
- Notes updated
- Comments added
- Mentions in notes
- Task assignments

## Troubleshooting

### Connection Issues

**Problem**: Authorization fails or redirects to an error page

**Solution**:
- Ensure you have admin access to the Slack workspace
- Check that your Slack workspace allows third-party app installations
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting the integration

### Missing Channels

**Problem**: Expected channels don't appear in the channel list

**Solution**:
- Verify the Slack app has access to the channels
- Check channel permissions and visibility settings
- Reconnect the integration to refresh the channel list

### Messages Not Posting

**Problem**: Notes aren't appearing in Slack channels

**Solution**:
- Verify the Slack app has posting permissions in the channel
- Check that the channel is public or the app is a member
- Review Slack workspace app settings and permissions

## Security & Privacy

- All communication with Slack is encrypted
- Your Slack tokens are securely stored and encrypted
- Epic Stack only accesses the permissions you explicitly grant
- You can revoke access at any time from Slack's app management

## Disconnecting

To disconnect Slack:

1. Go to organization settings
2. Navigate to **Integrations**
3. Find **Slack** and click **Disconnect**
4. Confirm the disconnection

You can also revoke access from your Slack workspace settings.
